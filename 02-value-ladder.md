## Value Ladder Framework Notes

The Value Ladder is a business framework (popularized by Russell Brunson, DotCom Secrets) that helps structure offers in ascending levels of value and price.

**Why It Works:**
- Builds trust incrementally
- Maximizes customer lifetime value
- Helps segment your audience
- Creates predictable revenue

**Key Stages:**
- **Lead Magnet (Bait):** Free value to build trust and capture leads
- **Frontend Offer:** Low-ticket product to convert leads to buyers
- **Core Offer:** Main product/service delivering full solution
- **Continuity Program:** Ongoing value through memberships or add-ons
- **Backend Offer:** Premium, high-ticket solutions for best customers
