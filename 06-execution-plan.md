## PART 1: Business Classification

### Business Model & Market

- **Business Type:** B2B
- **Market Position:** Profound and Otterly cater to enterprises but ignore local service nuances. No major player focuses on AI visibility for local SMBs.

### Target Audience

**Buyer Personas:**
- Local service business owners
- Digital marketing managers
- Small ecommerce business owners

**Key Pain Points:**
- Limited AI-driven visibility
- Inaccurate AI recommendations
- Lack of actionable insights for AI optimization

---

## PART 2: Phase 1 Roadmap (0–6 Months)

### Core Strategy

**MVP Approach:** An AI-powered tracker that provides visibility insights and improvement suggestions.

**Initial Offer:**
- Model: Freemium
- Price: Free tier + $99/month premium
- Value Proposition: See AI recommendations and fixes
- Fulfillment: Web/Mobile platform

### Lead Generation Strategy

**Lead Magnet:**
- Type & Format: Interactive Report — AI Visibility Audit
- Expected Conversion: 5–10%
- Value Demonstration: Detailed, actionable insights

### Acquisition Channels

| Channel | Frequency | Rationale | Content Format | Target Metrics |
|---------|-----------|-----------|----------------|----------------|
| Local SEO Forums | Weekly | Existing engagement on SEO issues | Discussion threads, Q&A | 1,000 visits/month |
| Facebook Groups | Daily | Large active base of SMB owners | Webinars, success stories | 500 signups/month |
| SEO | Monthly | High search intent | Blog posts, guides | 1,000 organic leads/month |

---

## PART 3: Phase 2 Roadmap (6–18 Months)

### Growth Strategy

**Traction Milestones:** Reach 5,000 active SMB users monitoring AI recommendations

**Expansion Plan:**
- Strategic Focus: Incorporate more AI engines
- Development Timeline: 4 months
- Pricing Evolution: Introduce tiered pricing based on features

**Go-to-Market Approach:** Leverage existing users for early testing

---

## PART 4: Implementation Plan

### Step-by-Step Execution

1. Develop a prototype using AI to track local business mentions
2. Implement initial AI integrations (ChatGPT, Google AA)
3. Launch the web platform with interactive dashboards
4. Gather feedback from early adopters and iterate
5. Expand AI integrations to include more platforms

### Success Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) | $20–$30 |
| Churn Rate | <5% |
| Pilot Conversion | 15% |

### Resource Requirements

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| Budget Range | $0–$10K |
| Team Structure | Solo founder with AI tools |
| Timeline | 1–2 weeks (MVP) |

### Risk Assessment & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Competition from established SEO tools | Innovate with unique AI insights |
| Rapid AI technology shifts | Regularly update platform capabilities |
| SMB awareness and education | Extensive content marketing |

### Next Action Steps

1. Develop MVP product using agentic IDEs
2. Launch on SEO forums and social platforms
3. Acquire early adopters through lead magnets and content marketing
